OUR INTIATIVES

Educational

Educational initiative focus on providing quality and learning oppertunies for all group.

we have established a smart board-equipped and internet-enabled classroom to enhance their learning experience. The primary objectives of this classroom include igniting students' interest in education through real-time e-learning experiences, utilizing various resources like audio and video materials, multimedia, images, online web conferences, PowerPoint presentations, and 2D & 3D animations.

DEEKSHA Foundation has established a Digital Literacy & Spoken English training Centre at its Kolkata activity center, Sarala Punnyashram. Here, children will receive computer training to improve their digital literacy skills, as well as enhance their communication skills through Spoken English training. This initiative aims to empower children with the necessary skills to navigate the digital world effectively and communicate confidently in English. A similar unit has been established in the forest village of Aushgram, Rangakhila, Purba Bardhaman district of West Bengal, extending these opportunities to Adivasi Children.

HEALTHCARE

Healthcare intiatives focus to provide access to essential health service,we also conduct health camp,awareness program and offers support for those in need.

We conduct regular free of cost Cervical Cancer Screening, VIA testing (visual inspection with acetic acid), PAP Smear test followed by HPV DNA testing was done, in collaboration with Chittaranjan National Cancer Institute for women aged between 30 to 60.

The Suswastha Kendras ensures quality healthcare is accessible to remote villager and the service of Specialist Doctors could be made available at the grass root level through Video Doctor Consultation..

DEEKSHA All people no matter where they live, no matter what their age, should have the right to access high quality and affordable healthcare and to lead healthy and productive lives.

ENVIROMENTAL

Enviromental intiatives strive to promoto sustainblity and enviromental awareness

DEEKSHA Foundation plants nearly 35000 saplings every year in different districts of West Bengal. Moreover, environmental awareness programmes are carried out once in 2 months.

This Foundation plants nearly 35000 saplings every year in different districts of West Bengal. Moreover, environmental awareness programmes are carried out once in 2 months. .

DEEKSHA Foundation has initiated a pilot project for reforestation in an area of about 5 bigha on the banks of Durgaduyani creek in Vidya Range, Bali Island, Sundarban National Park (West), Sundarban Tiger Reserve. Reforestation will be done in an area of 5 hectares in collaboration with the Department of Forest, Govt of West Bengal.
